
# Accenvix Solutions - Company Portal

## Overview
A bold and dynamic public website for **Accenvix Solutions**, a general tech services company. This is Phase 1 focusing on the public-facing website with a striking, energetic design that showcases the company's services and portfolio.

---

## Color & Design Direction
- **Primary**: Electric blue (#3B82F6) with deep navy accents
- **Accent**: Vibrant orange or cyan for CTAs and highlights
- **Style**: Bold typography, strong contrasts, dynamic animations, gradient backgrounds
- **Feel**: Energetic, modern, trustworthy

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Landing Page (Homepage)
- **Hero Section**: Full-width with animated gradient background, company tagline, and prominent CTA button
- **About Preview**: Brief intro to Accenvix Solutions with "Learn More" link
- **Services Grid**: Visual cards showcasing core services (development, consulting, support)
- **Why Choose Us**: Feature highlights with icons and bold stats
- **Contact CTA**: Eye-catching section encouraging visitors to get in touch

### 2. About Us Page
- Company story and mission
- Core values with dynamic icons
- Team section (placeholder for now)

### 3. Services Page
- Detailed breakdown of tech services offered
- Service cards with descriptions and icons
- Each service expandable for more details

### 4. Portfolio/Projects Page
- Grid of project showcases (static data initially)
- Project cards with images, titles, and brief descriptions
- Hover effects for engagement

### 5. Contact Page
- Contact form (Name, Email, Subject, Message)
- Form saves locally for now (database integration in Phase 2)
- Company contact information display
- Embedded map or location section

---

## Technical Approach
- **React + Vite + TypeScript** (Lovable's native stack)
- **Tailwind CSS** for styling with custom bold/dynamic theme
- **shadcn/ui** components for forms and UI elements
- **React Router** for navigation
- **Responsive design** for all screen sizes
- **Smooth animations** using Tailwind animate utilities

---
